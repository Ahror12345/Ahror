Different Parts

  • Deck/board
  • Griptape
  • Trucks
  • Wheels
  • Bearings
  • Riser
  • Hardware

Deck/Board:

The Skatebaord deck is the flat board that you stand on while riding. There are many different types of decks. Some types of deck are penny boards which are short decks, long boards which is the long one, and finally a regular deck ideal for tricks.

Griptape:

The grip tape is the gritty, that gets applied to the top of a skateboard deck so that your choices can have something to grip on while crusing and doing tricks. There are many different types of custom griptapes you can apply to your board.

Trucks:

Trucks are the T-shaped metal pieces that mount on the underside of your board. On the trucks is where you put your wheels and bearings.

Wheels:

The wheels goes on the truck and there are different types of wheels depending on your needs. For general crusing around in the city, you should go with larger rubber wheels which is easier for many people because of how easily it can get over cracks on the sidewalks. But if you want to just do tricks you should go with smaller wheels. They are better suited for smooth streets and skate parks.

Bearings:

The bearings is what you put inside of the wheels. They are used to make the wheels spin smoothly when they're on the trucks of the board. There are 2 bearings for each wheel making it total of 8 bearings.

Risers:

Risers are the hard plastic pads that is inserted between the trucks and the deck. They are used to avoid wheel white which can happen when the wheels rubs the skatebaord deck during a turn which can cause the skateboard to stop rolling and will cause you to fall.

Hardware:

Hardwares are the bolts and screws that go into the skateboard.
